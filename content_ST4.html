Mobilizing for the 2025 UN Ocean Conference

Date: October 21, 2024

The UN is preparing for the Third Ocean Conference 2025 to protect oceans globally. Experts call for more funding and action to achieve the Life Below Water goal, which remains one of the least funded SDGs.

Investing in SDG 14 to Sustain Ocean Action

Date: February 26, 2025

New discussions focus on how to fund ocean conservation projects. Public and private organizations are working together to invest in marine life protection and fight climate change through ocean action.

Toxic Algae Threatens Malibu Sea Lions

Date: February 25, 2025

Toxic algae blooms are causing severe illness in sea lions. Wildlife authorities and organizations are working to rescue affected animals and raise awareness to protect marine life from harmful algae outbreaks.

Oil Spill in the Black Sea Raises Environmental Concerns

Date: January 15, 2025

A damaged Russian tanker caused a large oil spill in the Black Sea, raising serious environmental concerns. Experts warn the spill could harm marine life and coastal ecosystems, calling for urgent cleanup efforts to prevent further damage.

Tasmanian Salmon Farms Hit by Bacterial Outbreak

Date: February 26, 2025

A bacterial outbreak is devastating Tasmania’s salmon farms, causing high fish mortality. Authorities are investigating, while environmental groups raise concerns over its impact on marine ecosystems.

Wildfire Debris Pollutes Los Angeles Beaches

Date: February 25, 2025

Los Angeles beaches are polluted with wildfire debris and urban runoff after heavy storms. Authorities are cleaning the beaches while warning the public about water contamination and threats to marine life.
