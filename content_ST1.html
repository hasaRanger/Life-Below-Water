Fun Facts about the Ocean and the life below

Ocean | Marine Life | Ecosystem

Ocean

The Ocean Has More Historical Artifacts Than Museums 🏺

It’s estimated that over 3 million shipwrecks are resting on the ocean floor, hiding untold stories!

The Ocean Produces Most of Our Oxygen 🌱


Over 50% of the oxygen we breathe comes from the ocean, thanks to phytoplankton, tiny marine plants that photosynthesize!

The Ocean Has Mountains & Waterfalls Too ⛰️💦

The tallest mountain on Earth is Mauna Kea (in Hawaii), which is mostly underwater and taller than Mount Everest! The biggest waterfall is also underwater – the Denmark Strait Cataract drops 3.5 times the height of Angel Falls!

Marine Life

The Blue Whale Is the Largest Animal Ever 🐋

The blue whale is bigger than any dinosaur, growing up to 100 feet long and weighing as much as 33 elephants!

There’s a Shark Older Than the USA 🦈

The Greenland Shark can live for over 500 years, meaning some sharks today were alive before the USA was founded (1776)!

There’s a Fish That Can “Walk” on Land 🐟🚶

The mudskipper is a fish that can survive outside of water, using its fins to "walk" on land!

Dolphins Call Each Other by “Names” 🐬🔊

Dolphins recognize each other using unique whistles—like calling each other by name!

Ecosystem

The Great Barrier Reef Is the Largest Living Structure 🪸

The Great Barrier Reef is so big it can be seen from space! It’s home to over 1,500 fish species and thousands of marine creatures.

Mangroves Are the Ocean’s Carbon Superheroes 🌱

Mangrove forests store more carbon than rainforests, helping fight climate change! 1

Plastic in the Ocean Outnumbers Fish by 2050 🚯

If pollution continues, scientists estimate there will be more plastic than fish in the ocean by 2050—so conservation is critical!
