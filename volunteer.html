Ocean Cleanup Drives

📍Los Angeles, California (Santa Monica Beach Cleanup)

🕐 Duration- 1 day to 1 week

👤 Volunteers aged 16+

☀ Best time to volunteer: Year round(Best:April-October)

Be a part of a world movement to remove plastic trash from beaches and seas. Plastic marine pollution hurts aquatic life and the ocean environment, and through your volunteering, you help preserve wildlife and healthy seas for future generations. Volunteer at cleanups within your community or start one individually in your hometown!


Coral Reef Restoration

📍Cairns, Queensland (Great Barrier Reef Restoration)

🕐 Duration- 1 week to 3 months

👤 Volunteers aged 18+(Scuba Certified)

☀ Best time to volunteer: March-November

The sea's rainforest is coral reef, but one that is also under threat due to climate change and pollution. The restoration of damaged coral reefs by planting pieces of coral and monitoring their progress is the focus of this initiative. Marine scientists and volunteer divers collaborate to help restore such life-giving systems.


Sea Turtle Protection

📍 Tortuguero (Sea Turtle Nesting Protection)

🕐 Duration- 2 weeks to 2 months

👤 Volunteers aged 16+(with Guardian) / 18+

☀ Best time to volunteer: June–October (Nesting Season)

Many sea turtle populations are under threat from extinction due to habitat destruction, pollution, and hunting. Volunteers help guard nesting sites ,rehabilitate injured turtles, and educate local communities on how to conserve these creatures. You can volunteer by beach watching, relocating eggs to safe zones, and support for turtle rehabilitation centers.


Sustainable Fisheries Projects

📍 Cebu (Fisheries Sustainability Programs)

🕐 Duration- 1 month to 6 months

👤 Volunteers aged 18+

☀ Best time to volunteer: March–August

Overfishing is one of the largest threats to marine diversity. This project sensitizes fishermen in fishing communities on using sustainable fishing methods, promotes marine protected areas, and aims to reduce bycatch. Volunteers can be involved in research, community outreach, and campaigns for sustainable seafood.


Restoration of Mangrove Forest

📍 Mombasa (Coastal Reforestation)

🕐 Duration- 1 week to 3 months

👤 Volunteers aged 15+

☀ Best time to volunteer: April–July, September–November

Mangroves are important in marine defense and carbon storage but are disappearing at an alarming rate. Volunteers help plant young mangrove seedlings, clean up trash along coastlines, and inform individuals about the important role of mangroves in sea creature defense and erosion prevention.


Monitoring of Oceans through Citizen Science

📍 Cornwall (Marine Biodiversity Research)

🕐 Duration- Flexible: 1 day to 3 months

👤 Volunteers aged 14+

☀ Best time to volunteer: May–September

Assist scientists in collecting vital information regarding ocean health, sea life, and levels of pollution. Volunteers may engage through water sampling, recording marine biodiversity, and submitting reports using apps and websites. Contributions enhance conservation policies and climate action plans.
